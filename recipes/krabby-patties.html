Krabby Patties

Description:

Although it may not be from an anime, this is an undeniable classic that has has generations foaming at the mouth. Everyone has been curious and wanted to know just what exactly one bite, a tiny morsel, of a Krabby Patty would taste like. No one cared that the Krusty Krab is underwater, it never mattered. All we see is the steaming buns, the delightful coloring, and the years of hype that the Krabby Patty has been known for.

Ingredients:

The ingredients needed for this meal seem to be:

  • Sesame seed buns
  • Beef patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Condiments
  • Pickles
  • The Krabby Patty Secret Formula

Steps:

I'm no fry cook, but I would assume the steps are as follows:

  1. Set ingredients down on workstation
  2. Slap patty on the grill
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Put condiments on the patty once cooked
  5. Tuck in the pickles with the cheese
  6. Carefully place the top bun
  7. Enjoy!
