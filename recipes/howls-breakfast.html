Calcifer's Breakfast

Description:

Watching Howl's Moving Castle is a great time but can quickly become torturous if you are on an empty stomach. Seeing such tasty animations of a classic breakfast, eggs and bacon, cooked to perfection by a snarky fire Demon can really work up your appetite and inspire you to go whip up your own batch of eggs and bacon!

Ingredients:

The ingredients needed for this meal seem to be:

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Fire Demon named Calcifer

Steps:

I'm no anime chef, but I would assume the steps are as follows:

  1. Set ingredients down on workstation
  2. Set bacon to cook
  3. Use bacon grease to grease up the pan
  4. Crack eggs into pan
  5. Feed the eggshells and scraps to Calcifer
  6. Plate once bacon and eggs are cooked
  7. Enjoy!
