Ramen Noodles

Description:

Everyone who has seen an anime knows that some of the most delightful, scrumptious, and tasty looking meals come from scenes in which the protagonists take a seat and dig into a bowl of their favorite meal. In this instance, I always felt that ramen noodles looked so good on screen and they never fail to make me want to reach in and grab a steaming serving for myself!

Ingredients:

The ingredients needed for this ramen seem to be:

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Pork
  • Egg
  • Scallions
  • Seasoning of choice

Steps:

I'm no anime chef, but I would assume the steps are as follows:

  1. Set ingredients down on workstation
  2. Set water to boil and set raw ingredients to the side
  3. Slice pork and begin to cook in pan
  4. Set egg to hardboil in pot
  5. Dice scallions
  6. Bring all of the ingredients into a bowl
  7. Enjoy!
